StructureDefinition: DAFAllergyIntolerance

The official URL for this profile is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/StructureDefinition/allergyintolerance-daf-dafallergyintolerance

Defines constraints and extensions on the AllergyIntolerance resource for use in querying and retrieving allergy information.

This profile was published on Thu, Aug 21, 2014 00:00+0000 as a draft by U.S. Office of the National Coordinator (ONC).

Summary

Terminology Bindings

  • AllergyIntolerance.status SHALL come from Value set for AllergyIntolerance Status.
  • AllergyIntolerance.event.manifestation SHALL come from Clinical symptoms and/or signs that are observed or associated with an Adverse Reaction Event (Extensible)

Required/Prohibited Elements

  • The element AllergyIntolerance.identifier is required
  • The element AllergyIntolerance.patient is required
  • The element AllergyIntolerance.substance is required
  • The element AllergyIntolerance.event.manifestation is required
