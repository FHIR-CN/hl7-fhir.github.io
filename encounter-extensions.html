Encounter HL7 Extensions (Profile)

Defines common extensions used with or related to the Encounter resource
Extensions:
encounter-admissionTime of admission :

Time of admission, for hospitalizations only.

encounter-dischargeTime of discharge :

Time of discharge, for hospitalization encounters only.

encounter-reasonCancelledExplanation for cancellation :

If the encountered was cancelled after it was planned, why? Applies only if the status is cancelled.

encounter-primaryConditionCondition driving this encounter :

The condition first reported or highest in importance, from the clinical perspective.

encounter-relatedConditionOther associated conditions :

The conditions considered and cared for within this encounter. This includes items such as reason for visit, admission diagnosis, discharge diagnosis, chief complaint. Do not use reason or indication in the Action class to specify encounter-related diagnoses.

encounter-conditionA condition related to this encounter :

The reference to conditions related to this encounter, aside from the primary condition.

encounter-conditionRoleThe role of the condition :

The role of the condition within this encounter, e.g., chief complaint, admission diagnosis, discharge diagnosis, comorbidity.

encounter-associatedEncounterAssociated Encounter :

This encounter occurs within the scope of the referenced encounter.
